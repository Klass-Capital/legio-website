The problem

Your team are doing two jobs.
Only one of them is safety.

Every incident generates a trail of reports, classifications, notifications, and regulatory obligations. Legacy platforms didn't eliminate that trail — they digitized it. Officers still carry it.

Legio automates the outcomes that can be automated — documentation, compliance, reporting — so your team can focus on the outcomes that cannot be automated — physical presence, judgment, response.

How Legio works

Your officers stay in charge.
Legio handles what follows.

1

Officer reports the event

Structured, guided intake on mobile or desktop. The right fields, for the right incident type — nothing more.

2

Legio routes and classifies automatically

Incident type determines workflow. Reportable events are flagged, categorized, and queued.

3

Notifications go to the right people

Supervisors, leadership, legal — whoever needs to know, gets notified. Based on rules you set once.

4

Regulatory obligations close out automatically

ASR data populates from operations, not memory. Your annual security report writes itself from the events your team already captured.

The platform

Every function of safety and security operations. One system.

Legio replaces the disconnected tools most departments are running today — each module tied to the same operational record.

Guard Tour
Real-time patrol visibility
Every checkpoint hit. Every missed patrol flagged. Supervisors see the full picture as it happens — not at the end of shift.
Incident Management
Captured, routed, and closed.
From first report to final classification. Every incident handled end to end — without manual follow-up from your team.
Regulatory Compliance
Reports write themselves.

Dispatch
Officers dispatched. Response tracked.
Assign officers, log response times, and track outcomes. Every dispatch creates a record — so you always know who went, when, and what happened.

Current policy always accessible. Acknowledgement tracked. No printing, no binders, no guessing what version is live.
