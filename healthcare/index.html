Healthcare Security

Your team manages risk that most security programs never see. Legio is built for that.

Healthcare security teams operate in one of the most demanding environments in the world. High incident volume, elevated risk, and regulatory obligations that don't stop. Legio eliminates the administrative burden so your team can focus on the floor.

The environment

Your team manages risk that most security programs never see.

Healthcare security officers deal with a different category of threat — patients in crisis, visitors under stress, high-stakes environments where incidents escalate quickly. The administrative burden that follows every event compounds the pressure.

Legio eliminates the paperwork without changing the protocol. Your officers focus on the environment. The documentation is taken care of.

Higher violence risk
Healthcare workers face a disproportionate share of workplace violence incidents. The security program needs to document, respond to, and prevent them — simultaneously.
Regulatory obligations that don't pause
Joint Commission standards require documentation that most security teams are still producing manually. The survey doesn't wait for a convenient time.
Administrative burden follows every event
Every incident creates reporting work. In an environment where incidents happen daily, that burden accumulates fast — and it comes directly from time that should be spent on the floor.

"Healthcare security teams are doing two jobs. The job of keeping people safe, and the job of documenting that they did. Only one of those jobs should exist."

What Legio does

Less administration. More security.

Purpose-built for the demands of healthcare security. Incident management, compliance, and operations that work the way your team works.

Incident Management

Every incident captured completely. No re-entry. No follow-up paperwork.

Structured intake for the full range of healthcare security events — assaults, patient behavioural incidents, elopements, code situations. Officers capture it once. Classification, routing, and documentation run automatically from there.

Workplace Violence

The documentation your WPV program requires — built from operations, not from memory.

Workplace violence incidents classified and documented at point of entry. Trends visible in real time. The reporting your prevention program depends on is produced automatically — without a separate workflow.

Joint Commission

Survey-ready without a preparation sprint.

Environment of Care documentation, patrol records, sentinel event logs — structured continuously from daily operations. When a survey is scheduled, the record is already there. No scramble, no reconstruction, no gaps.

Investigations

Incidents that need follow-up get the structure to support it.

When an incident requires investigation, the record is already built. Assign investigators, track findings, log actions — all connected to the original event without starting over in a separate system.
